Understanding Your Legal Rights and Options

Child Custody Legal Aid helps you understand legal documents, advice and referral services. Child Custody Legal Aid is also referred to by some of its recognized programs, including: Children First Always, Fathers for Equal Rights and Texas Volunteer Attorneys.

We join together to understand and help you. Please, contact us.

Our Programs

Children First Always

Dedicated to ensuring your children's best interests come first in all custody matters.

Fathers for Equal Rights

Supporting fathers' rights and ensuring equal treatment in custody proceedings.

Texas Volunteer Attorneys

Connecting families with experienced legal professionals who can provide guidance.

What We Provide

  • Help understanding legal documents related to child custody
  • Legal advice and consultation services
  • Referrals to licensed attorneys who specialize in family law
  • Resources and information about your rights as a parent
  • Support throughout the legal process
