Welcome to Child Custody Legal Aid

Child Custody Legal Aid is a program provided by the America Family Law Center, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit charitable organization. We are committed to helping you ensure that your children's best interests come first.

If an individual is involved in any type of a child custody dispute or is attempting to gain access to their children, Child Custody Legal Aid may help. Contact Us today.

Our mission is to help parents understand and navigate their child custody situation for the purpose of benefiting the children. We understand that these matters can be emotionally challenging, and we're here to provide support and guidance you need.
