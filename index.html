Welcome to Child Custody Legal Aid

If an individual is involved in any type of a child custody dispute or is attempting to gain access to their children, Child Custody Legal Aid can help you complete the necessary paperwork in matters related to child custody.

As a program provided by the America Family Law Center, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit charitable organization, we are dedicated to ensuring that your children's best interests come first.

How We Can Help

  • Legal document preparation and assistance
  • Guidance through child custody procedures
  • Referrals to licensed attorneys when needed
  • Resources and information about your legal rights

Our mission is to provide accessible legal aid services to families navigating the complexities of child custody in Texas. We understand that these matters can be emotionally challenging, and we're here to provide the support and guidance you need.
